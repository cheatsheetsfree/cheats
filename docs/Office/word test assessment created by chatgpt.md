**Microsoft Word Beginner-Intermediate Test Assessment**

**Section 1: Basic Functionality**

**Q1.** What is the default file extension for a Microsoft Word
document?

A\) .doc\
B) .docx\
C) .txt\
D) .pdf

**Answer:**\
B) .docx

**Q2.** How can you open the \"Find\" dialog box to search for a word or
phrase in a document?

A\) Press Ctrl + F\
B) Press Ctrl + S\
C) Press Ctrl + P\
D) Press Ctrl + A

**Answer:**\
A) Press Ctrl + F

**Q3.** Which of the following is NOT a valid text alignment option in
Microsoft Word?

A\) Left Align\
B) Right Align\
C) Center Align\
D) Split Align

**Answer:**\
D) Split Align

**Section 2: Formatting and Styles**

**Q4.** To change the font size in a Word document, you would click on
the \_\_\_\_ in the Home tab.

A\) Font Style dropdown\
B) Font Size dropdown\
C) Font Color button\
D) Text Effects button

**Answer:**\
B) Font Size dropdown

**Q5.** Which keyboard shortcut is used to bold selected text in
Microsoft Word?

A\) Ctrl + B\
B) Ctrl + I\
C) Ctrl + U\
D) Ctrl + E

**Answer:**\
A) Ctrl + B

**Q6.** What does the \"Styles\" group in the \"Home\" tab in Microsoft
Word allow you to do?

A\) Change the color of text\
B) Apply predefined formatting to headings and paragraphs\
C) Spell-check your document\
D) Insert images into your document

**Answer:**\
B) Apply predefined formatting to headings and paragraphs

**Section 3: Page Layout and Document Tools**

**Q7.** How do you insert a page break in Microsoft Word?

A\) Press Ctrl + Enter\
B) Click on \"Insert\" tab \> \"Page Break\"\
C) Both A and B\
D) Click on the \"Layout\" tab \> \"Page Break\"

**Answer:**\
C) Both A and B

**Q8.** Which of the following is a way to change the orientation of a
page from portrait to landscape?

A\) Click on \"Page Layout\" \> \"Orientation\" \> \"Landscape\"\
B) Click on \"Insert\" \> \"Page Orientation\" \> \"Landscape\"\
C) Use the \"View\" tab \> \"Orientation\"\
D) Press Ctrl + L

**Answer:**\
A) Click on \"Page Layout\" \> \"Orientation\" \> \"Landscape\"

**Q9.** To insert a header or footer into a document, where would you
go?

A\) Click on the \"Insert\" tab \> \"Header\" or \"Footer\"\
B) Click on the \"Design\" tab \> \"Header\" or \"Footer\"\
C) Click on the \"View\" tab \> \"Header\" or \"Footer\"\
D) Click on the \"Home\" tab \> \"Insert\"

**Answer:**\
A) Click on the \"Insert\" tab \> \"Header\" or \"Footer\"

**Section 4: Tables and Lists**

**Q10.** How can you quickly create a numbered list in Microsoft Word?

A\) Click on the \"Insert\" tab \> \"Numbered List\"\
B) Press Ctrl + N\
C) Click on the \"Home\" tab \> \"Numbering\"\
D) Press Ctrl + Shift + N

**Answer:**\
C) Click on the \"Home\" tab \> \"Numbering\"

**Q11.** If you want to insert a table into your document, which tab
would you use?

A\) Insert\
B) Home\
C) Table\
D) Layout

**Answer:**\
A) Insert

**Q12.** You can resize a table by dragging the borders of its columns
or rows. True or False?

**Answer:**\
True

**Section 5: Advanced Features (Intermediate Level)**

**Q13.** What is the purpose of the \"Track Changes\" feature in
Microsoft Word?

A\) To keep track of spelling and grammar mistakes\
B) To track changes made to the document, including insertions,
deletions, and formatting\
C) To track the time spent editing the document\
D) To save previous versions of the document

**Answer:**\
B) To track changes made to the document, including insertions,
deletions, and formatting

**Q14.** To add a citation to a Word document, which tab would you use?

A\) References\
B) Home\
C) Insert\
D) Review

**Answer:**\
A) References

**Q15.** What is a \"SmartArt\" graphic in Word?

A\) A type of chart used for financial data\
B) A predefined shape or visual representation of information, like
lists or processes\
C) A tool to make text bold\
D) A high-quality image used in the document

**Answer:**\
B) A predefined shape or visual representation of information, like
lists or processes

**Q16.** How can you protect a document by setting a password?

A\) Click on \"File\" \> \"Info\" \> \"Protect Document\" \> \"Encrypt
with Password\"\
B) Click on \"Home\" \> \"Password Settings\"\
C) Go to \"Insert\" \> \"Password\"\
D) Go to \"Review\" \> \"Protect\"

**Answer:**\
A) Click on \"File\" \> \"Info\" \> \"Protect Document\" \> \"Encrypt
with Password\"

**Section 6: Final Review**

**Q17.** To print a document, which keyboard shortcut can you use?

A\) Ctrl + P\
B) Ctrl + S\
C) Ctrl + T\
D) Ctrl + Q

**Answer:**\
A) Ctrl + P

**Q18.** How can you check the word count of a document in Word?

A\) Click \"File\" \> \"Properties\"\
B) Go to \"Review\" \> \"Word Count\"\
C) Click \"Insert\" \> \"Word Count\"\
D) Click \"Home\" \> \"Word Count\"

**Answer:**\
